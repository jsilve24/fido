# Data from Silverman et al. (2018) Microbiome

High Resolution (hourly and daily) sampling of 4 in vitro artificial gut
models with many technical replicates to identify technical variation.

## Usage

``` r
data(mallard_family)
```

## Format

A list containing "otu_table", "sample_data", "tax_table", and "refseq".

## Details

This data is at the family level and processed as in Silverman et al.
2018. Data at the sequence variant level without preprocessing is given
in [`mallard`](https://jsilve24.github.io/fido/reference/mallard.md)

## References

Silverman et al. "Dynamic linear models guide design and analysis of
microbiota studies within artificial human guts". Microbiome 2018 6:202
